Tucked in an alley in the heart of Athens, Birdman is an intimate, no-fuss-no-frills, Japanese bar & grill,
that pays homage to late-night Tokyo eateries and jazz Kissa-style listening bars in Japan, serving new
style yakitori, straightforward drinks, and Hi-Fi analog tunes.
